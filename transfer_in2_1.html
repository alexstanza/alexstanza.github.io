Transfer from blockchain wallet

To transfer dSGD from an external blockchain wallet into your disc Account, use your public Ethereum address available below. Avoid typing addresses, tap the field below to copy it instead.

Important: Funds transferred to the wrong address might be lost permanently. Sending any token other than dSGD to this address will result in funds being lost forever. Only send amounts using two decimal places because after that you won't be credited (e.g. sending 1.009999 results in you getting 1.00).
